Welcome to KPR Elect!

KPR Elect is the official online platform for conducting student council elections. Every vote contributes to selecting responsible student leaders who will represent and serve the student community. Your vote is secure, confidential, and important. Please read the election guidelines carefully before proceeding.

Election Guidelines

  • Every student can vote only once.
  • Your vote is confidential and secure.
  • Select one candidate for each position.
  • Once your vote is submitted, it cannot be changed.
  • Do not share your login credentials with anyone.
  • Vote honestly and responsibly for deserving candidates.
